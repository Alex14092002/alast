Đặc trưng

  • This product is a heat exhaustion heat index meter with a set of tresability documents (4-piece set of calibration transcript, calibration certificate, system chart, and reference instrument transcript) attached.
  • Calibration Standard Point Temperature 23°C (Not Black Ball Temperature) Humidity 60% (at23°C)
  • Handy type measuring instrument with integrated Black Ball Thermometer, Thermometer and Hygrometer, which can be used as a guideline for preventing heat stroke and as an evaluation of heat stress in the working environment to measure WBGT index.
  • Use this product to prevent heatstroke by observing the wind chill (heat).
  • The unit complies with JIS B 7922 Class 2 for physical education, club activities, extracurricular activities, indoor and outdoor sports events, work sites in high temperature environments, roads, maintenance work, and agricultural work.
  • When the setting value with alarm alarm (any WBGT index) is reached, it is announced by buzzer.
  • With HOLD function. It also has a function to display the highest value (MAX) and lowest value (MIN).
  • WBGT index measurement environment setting and auto power off (about 20 minutes) function are included.
  • Comes with a hard case that is convenient to carry and store
  • Standard sticker The WBGT index guideline for preventing heatstroke is used as a standard sticker. Select one of three types ((1) Japan Sports Association (2) the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare (3) Japan Industrial Hygiene Society) depending on the use, and attach it to the front of the unit (lower part of the key).
  • There is also a separate tripod that can be installed at a height similar to that of a human. (Optional) No. 8310-80 M45
  • To prevent the measurement temperature from being affected by radiation heat, measure the temperature in an area where there is nothing nearby.
  • It is possible to install "Radiation Protection Parts (Option No. 8310-20)" to protect the temperature and humidity detection part and the main body case part from the radiant heat of the heat source.
