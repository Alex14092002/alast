Đặc trưng

  • Long stroke compatible stepping drive objective lens actuator.
  • Since it uses a stepping motor, it can be connected to various controllers of the company.
  • Its small size and high resolution make it ideal for integration into microscope cylinders and autofocus systems.
  • Can be used with upright and inverted microscopes.
  • A special adapter (part number: OBL-ADP3-M ***) is available to suit the size of the microscope and objective lens screws of each manufacturer.
  • * A dedicated adapter is required for attaching to the microscope and objective lens.
