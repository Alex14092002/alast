Đặc trưng

  • A data logger connected to the cloud that measures and records temperature and humidity. By using the free cloud service "Ondotori Web Storage," you can check the temperature and humidity "from anywhere, anytime," whether you use a smartphone ora computer.

Thông số kỹ thuật

  • What to measure: Temperature, humidity
  • Measuring range: - 25 ~ 70 degrees C: 0 ~ 99% RH: (-20 degrees C to 70 degrees C)
  • Primary accuracy: +/- 0.3 degrees C +/- 2.5% RH at 15 ~ 35 degrees C, 30 ~ 80% RH
  • Display: Measured value, battery life warning, etc.
  • Data storage capacity: 30,000 x 2 ch
  • Recording interval: 15 different ways: 1 second to 60 minutes
  • Recording mode: endless/one-time
  • Communications: Wireless LAN, USB communication, Bluetooth 4.2 (Bluetooth Low Energy)
  • How to check alerts: Email, Browser
  • Power: 2 AA alkalis, AC adapter, USB bus power
  • Operating environment: Temperature: - 10 ~ 60 deg. C Humidity: 90% RH or less No condensation
  • Size: H 58 mm x W 78 mm x D 26 mm
  • Waterproof: None
  • Battery life: About 10 days to 15 months
  • Includes: 1 x temperature and humidity sensor (SHA -3151), AA alkaline battery (LR 6), registration code label, and complete instruction manual (with warranty)
  • Calibration points are 1 point each at 25 degrees Celsius and 50%. If you would like additional points, please contact us separately.
